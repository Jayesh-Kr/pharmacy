# Pharmacy DBMS Dashboard & Navigation Guide

The left-side navigation panel provides quick access to all essential modules of the Pharmacy Database Management System. Below is a breakdown of each section and its specific use case:

## 1. Dashboard
- **Use Case:** Provides a high-level overview of the pharmacy's performance and current status.
- **Key Features:** Key metrics (total medicines, revenue, customer count, low-stock alerts), recent revenue charts, and top-selling medicine trends.

## 2. Medicines
- **Use Case:** A unified catalog for all products available in the pharmacy.
- **Key Features:** Add, update, or remove medicines; manage categories; set pricing and generic names.

## 3. Inventory
- **Use Case:** Deep-dive into stock levels, batches, and item tracking.
- **Key Features:** Monitor low-stock or out-of-stock items, manage incoming stock batches, and track expiration dates to prevent dispensing expired products.

## 4. Sales & POS
- **Use Case:** The core retail interface (Point of Sale) for daily operations.
- **Key Features:** Bill generation, scanning or searching items to sell, calculating totals with taxes/discounts, and viewing recent transaction history.

## 5. Prescriptions
- **Use Case:** Keep track of patient prescriptions and fulfill them directly.
- **Key Features:** Log doctor prescriptions tied to specific patients, ensuring controlled substances are safely dispensed and monitored.

## 6. Customers
- **Use Case:** Patient/Customer relationship management.
- **Key Features:** Record customer details, view their purchase history, and maintain contact info for prescription refills or follow-ups.

## 7. Doctors
- **Use Case:** Directory of medical professionals.
- **Key Features:** Track doctors who frequently route prescriptions to the pharmacy for validation and record-keeping.

## 8. Suppliers
- **Use Case:** Manage vendors and distributors.
- **Key Features:** Store contact details, terms of trade, and view delivery history for the companies that provide your pharmacy's stock.

## 9. Purchases
- **Use Case:** Track incoming stock and purchase orders from suppliers.
- **Key Features:** Log new stock shipments, update inventory quantities automatically upon receiving a purchase, and track wholesale costs.

## 10. User Management (Admin Only)
- **Use Case:** Staff control and access management.
- **Key Features:** Add new pharmacists or cashiers, assign roles, and revoke access for departed employees.
